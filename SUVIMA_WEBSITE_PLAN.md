# SUVIMA - Website Planning Document

**Supporting Understanding Via Information for Medical Awareness**

## Project Overview

**Domain:** suvima.org (suvima.com is taken)  
**Purpose:** Umbrella educational website for neurodevelopmental and pediatric conditions  
**Target Audience:** Parents, young people, and healthcare professionals  
**Approach:** Condition-based navigation with age-appropriate content

## Content Principles

### Evidence-Based
- All information based on current research
- References to NICE guidelines where applicable
- Regular content review and updates
- Clear distinction between established and emerging evidence

### Neurodiversity-Affirming
- Respectful language
- Strength-based approach
- Avoid deficit-focused narratives
- Celebrate diversity

### Accessible
- Plain language throughout
- Glossary for medical terms
- Multiple reading levels
- Visual aids and diagrams
- Video content where helpful

### Age-Appropriate
- Content tailored to developmental stage
- Direct address to young people in teen/young adult sections
- Parent-focused for younger age groups

### Culturally Sensitive
- Inclusive examples
- Consideration of diverse family structures
- Awareness of cultural differences in understanding conditions

## Design & User Experience

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Blues (trust, calm, medical professionalism)
- **Secondary:** Teals (healthcare, support, growth)
- **Accent:** Warm tones (approachability, hope)
- **Neutral:** Warm grays (readability, professional)

### Typography
- **Headers:** Serif font (Crimson Pro or similar) - warmth and professionalism
- **Body:** Sans-serif (DM Sans or similar) - clarity and readability
- **Accessibility:** Minimum 16px body text, high contrast

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- Touch-friendly navigation
- Readable on all devices
- Fast loading times

### Navigation
- Sticky header for easy access
- Breadcrumb trails
- Clear "back to" links
- Search functionality
- Related content suggestions

## Technical Considerations

### Platform Options
1. **WordPress** - Easy content management, extensive plugins
2. **Custom React/Next.js** - Full control, modern experience
3. **Static Site Generator** (e.g., Hugo, Jekyll) - Fast, secure, simple

### Hosting
- Netlify or similar for static sites
- WordPress hosting if using WP
- CDN for fast global delivery

### SEO Optimization
- Meta descriptions for all pages
- Structured data markup
- XML sitemap
- Mobile-friendly
- Fast page load
- Quality backlinks

### Analytics
- Privacy-focused analytics (Plausible or similar)
- User journey tracking
- Content popularity
- Search queries
- No invasive tracking

### Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance minimum
- Screen reader compatible
- Keyboard navigation
- Alt text for all images
- Captions for videos

## Content Governance

### Editorial Process
1. Content creation by medical professionals
2. Review by specialists in relevant field
3. Plain language check
4. Neurodiversity-affirming language check
5. Fact-checking and reference verification
6. Family review panel feedback
7. Publication
8. Regular review (minimum annually)

### Version Control
- Date stamps on all content
- "Last reviewed" dates visible
- Archive of previous versions
- Change log for major updates

### Sources & Citations
- Clear references throughout
- Links to original research where appropriate
- NICE guidelines prominently featured
- Distinction between guidelines and emerging evidence
